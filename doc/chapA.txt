  [1XA.1 [33X[0;0YThe different components[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0Y[5XFinInG[105X  consists  of the following components: geometry, liegeometry, group,
  projectivespace,  correlations,  polarspace/morphisms, enumerators, diagram,
  varieties,  affinespace/affinegroup, gpolygons, and orbits-stabilisers. Each
  of  these  components corresponds with a [13Xcomponent.gd[113X and [13Xcomponent.gi[113X file.
  The  file  [13Xcomponent.gi[113X will be dependent on [13Xcomponent.gd[113X and all previously
  loaded  [13X.gd[113X  files,  [13Xcomponent1/component2[113X means that both [13Xcomponent1.gi[113X and
  [13Xcomponent2.gi[113X   depend   on  the  declarations  in  both  [13Xcomponent1.gd[113X  and
  [13Xcomponent2.gd[113X.[133X
